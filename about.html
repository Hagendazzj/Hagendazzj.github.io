About me

As 8 year old kid I would come home and either play with Photoshop or take apart electronics in the garage. I found worlds in both these things. As a teenager I was making angsty experimental videos with Final Cut. After that I found music and delved into making music with friends. Later I was working in marketing and sales and I found myself asking why certain design decisions were made and curious how these decisions would connect to the whole customer experience. I’m so grateful I’ ve found something that I can connect with on a deep level.

Thanks for dropping by and I look forward to hearing from you soon!
